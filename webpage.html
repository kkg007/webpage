Computer Virus


VIRUS stands for Vital Infomation Resource Under Seize,viruses are programs which are created deliberately to damage critical information and data. The programmer of a virus known as Virus Author, intentionally writes a virus program with a motive to damage data or programs residing in a computer. Some virus attaches itself to a file and lies dormant.But when a certain date or event occurs, it gets triggered and become active. One such notorious virus is called Friday The 13th.It is also called Jerusalem Virusbecause, it was first discovered at the university of Jerusalem in 1887.It gets activated every Friday that occurs on the 13th of a month.
